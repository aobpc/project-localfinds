## Retro

### Good

- PlantUML was easy to understand and utilize.
- Flask made routing additional webpages simple.
- Pytest made testing routes simple.
- Werkzeug security package made encrypting passwords simple.

### Bad

- CSS is difficult to perfect.
- Website does not look correct on mobile.
- Post ownership is tied to username instead of account.

### Actionable Commitments

- As a team, we will continue making progress on this website. Focusing on mobile compatibility and updating ownership of posts with the change of username. Furthermore, integration testing will be implemented between the routes databases.
